Built on trust

Your vault key never leaves your browser

Your policies are encrypted with a personal vault key that only you know. We never store it, never transmit it, and have no way to read your data without it.

We can't see your policies

All policy data stored in our database is encrypted. Without your vault key, it's unreadable — even to us. If you lose your key, we can't recover your data.

Documents auto-deleted

Uploaded PDFs are processed and automatically deleted within 15 minutes. We don't keep copies of your policy documents.
